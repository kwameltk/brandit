Our Story

Brands Clinic was borne from a Tourism agency LTk Tours which is ensuring that travelers who visit Kwahu , have access to product and services. This new venture which is a subsidiary , will guides businesses toward digital success. We offer a tailored approach that combines strategic branding with practical solutions, including enhanced online visibility, robust social media presence, and exceptional customer service with focus on Virtual Assistant service. We empower businesses to connect meaningfully with their audience and realize their full potential in the digital landscape.

Our Mission: To empower businesses to thrive in the digital world by providing expert marketing and branding solutions.

Our Vision: To be the leading provider of innovative and results-driven digital marketing services.

Our Services

Google Business Profile

We create and optimize Google My Business profiles for local businesses, helping them gain visibility in Google search results and maps.
Why It Matters: Many customers start their shopping experience with a Google search. By having a properly set up and optimized profile, businesses are more likely to attract local customers.

WhatsApp Catalog

We set up a WhatsApp Business profile with a product/service catalog, making it easy for customers to browse and order directly through chat.
Why It Matters: WhatsApp is widely used in local communities. A catalog on WhatsApp allows businesses to engage directly with customers and simplify the order process.

Virtual Assistant

Brands Clinic empowers businesses to scale their operations by providing highly skilled virtual assistants specializing in vendor management, customer service, data analysis, and content creation .

Why It Matters: Our trained VAs handle essential back-office tasks, freeing up business owners to focus on strategic growth and expansion. Read Our Profile

.

Fast Delivery Service

We partner with local delivery services and help integrate logistics solutions into their operations, allowing businesses to offer home delivery options to customers.
Why It Matters: Customers are more likely to buy if delivery is available. For businesses, this increases reach without the need for physical stores.

Customer Service

We help you set up and manage effective customer service channels to ensure your clients receive prompt and professional responses.
Why It Matters:Good customer service is the backbone of a successful business. Quick and efficient communication fosters customer loyalty, increases retention, and improves your business’s reputation.

Online Media Management

We manage your social media presence by creating content, scheduling posts, and engaging with your audience. This keeps your brand active, visible, and relevant to potential and current customers across platforms like Facebook, Instagram, and more.
Why It Matters: In today’s digital age, social media is where customers connect with brands.
